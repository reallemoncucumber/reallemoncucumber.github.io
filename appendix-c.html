Mastering Roguelike Game Design: From Permadeath to Procedural Generation

Appendix C: Bibliography and Further Reading

This appendix provides a comprehensive list of references and additional resources for readers who want to deepen their understanding of roguelike game design and development. The list includes books, academic papers, online articles, and other relevant materials.

Books

  1. Barton, M. (2008). Dungeons and Desktops: The History of Computer Role-Playing Games. A K Peters/CRC Press.

  2. Brewer, N. (2021). Procedural Storytelling in Game Design. CRC Press.

  3. Dormans, J. (2017). Procedural Content Generation for Game Design. Addison-Wesley Professional.

  4. Ebert, D. S., Musgrave, F. K., Peachey, D., Perlin, K., & Worley, S. (2003). Texturing and Modeling: A Procedural Approach. Morgan Kaufmann.

  5. Gamma, E., Helm, R., Johnson, R., & Vlissides, J. (1994). Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software. Addison-Wesley.

  6. Nystrom, R. (2014). Game Programming Patterns. Genever Benning.

  7. Shaker, N., Togelius, J., & Nelson, M. J. (2016). Procedural Content Generation in Games. Springer.

  8. Sweetser, P. (2008). Emergence in Games. Charles River Media.

Academic Papers

  1. Ashlock, D., Lee, C., & McGuinness, C. (2011). Search-Based Procedural Generation of Maze-Like Levels. IEEE Transactions on Computational Intelligence and AI in Games, 3(3), 260-273.

  2. Dahlskog, S., & Togelius, J. (2012). Patterns and Procedural Content Generation: Revisiting Mario in World 1 Level 1. In Proceedings of the Workshop on Design Patterns in Games.

  3. Hendrikx, M., Meijer, S., Van Der Velden, J., & Iosup, A. (2013). Procedural Content Generation for Games: A Survey. ACM Transactions on Multimedia Computing, Communications, and Applications (TOMM), 9(1), 1-22.

  4. Johnson, L., Yannakakis, G. N., & Togelius, J. (2010). Cellular automata for real-time generation of infinite cave levels. In Proceedings of the 2010 Workshop on Procedural Content Generation in Games.

  5. Liapis, A., Yannakakis, G. N., & Togelius, J. (2015). Constrained Novelty Search: A Study on Game Content Generation. Evolutionary Computation, 23(1), 101-129.

  6. Smith, G., Whitehead, J., & Mateas, M. (2010). Tanagra: A Mixed-Initiative Level Design Tool. In Proceedings of the Fifth International Conference on the Foundations of Digital Games.

Online Articles and Tutorials

  1. Adonaac. (2015). "Procedural Dungeon Generation Algorithm." Blog post

  2. Bucklew, J. (2010). "The Complete Guide to (JRPG) Item Randomization." Blog post

  3. Doucet, L. (2016). "Rogue-like Dungeon Generation." Blog post

  4. Harris, J. (2016). "Understanding Pac-Man Ghost Behavior." Blog post

  5. Sylvester, T. (2013). "Designing Game Mechanics From a Rational Basis." Blog post

  6. Welsh, T. (2014). "Line of Sight Calculation in 2D." Blog post

Video Lectures and Talks

  2. Cousins, B. (2018). "Randomness: From Pokemon to Rogue-likes." GDC Talk

  4. Yu, D. (2016). "Generating Levels For a Daily Challenge." GDC Talk
